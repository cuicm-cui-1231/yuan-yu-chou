想说的话

Every time I see you, my heart skips a beat.

Your smile is my favorite sight, your voice my favorite sound.

I can not stop thinking about you—you’re all I want.

I’m falling for you, and I don’t want to hold it back anymore.

Will you let me love you.

青衿案侧墨痕新，
几度停笔复沉吟。
最是春风解人意，
偷翻诗页见卿名。
